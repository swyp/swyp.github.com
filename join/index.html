Join Team Swÿp

People like to share content. Swÿp is a framework for gestural content transfer, regardless of the platform on which implemented.

The Problem

Manufacturers have been creating silos of cooperability, in which devices can transfer content using novel methods like Photo Stream or Android Beam, but where outside devices collaborate using archaic methods like email. The siloing that is happening across vendor boundaries will block innovation and competition in the long term.

The Solution

Swÿp allows content transfer across any apps running on any devices, mediating content-type translation to provide seamless communication with a device-appropriate gesture. Here's one example.

What we're doing

We're promoting Swÿp as the standard for inter-device content transfer, designing new augmented reality techniques for transferring content, building display-free devices for physical-to-digital Swÿps, putting interactive digital displays around campus, and implementing a permanent Swÿp installation at the Media Lab.

Who we are

Who we're needing

  5. People interested in 3D interfaces and augmented reality, and capable of writing vision software for developing Swÿp as a sort of 'right-click for the air.'
